🎮 Match&Play Privacy Policy

We respect your privacy. Here's what we collect and why.

Last updated: March 2025

📋 Overview

Match&Play ("we", "our", "the app") is a social platform for gamers to find teammates. This Privacy Policy explains what personal data we collect, how we use it, and your rights regarding that data. By using the app, you agree to the terms described here.

📦 Data We Collect

  • Account info: Nickname, age, gender, avatar photo, bio, and selected games with ranks.
  • Authentication data: Email address (via Google Sign-In or Apple Sign-In).
  • Messages: Chat messages between matched users. All messages are encrypted end-to-end on our servers.
  • Usage data: Swipes (likes/passes), match activity, and lobby participation.
  • Device tokens: Firebase Cloud Messaging tokens for push notifications.
  • Purchase data: Subscription status (via RevenueCat). We do not store credit card or payment information.
  • Reports & blocks: If you report or block another user, we store that action to enforce safety.

🎯 How We Use Your Data

  • To show you other players' profiles for matchmaking.
  • To deliver and display chat messages between matched users.
  • To send push notifications about new messages and matches.
  • To enforce community safety (blocks, reports, moderation).
  • To manage premium subscriptions and feature access.
  • To improve and debug the app.

🔒 Data Storage & Security

Your data is stored securely on Supabase infrastructure (PostgreSQL database with Row Level Security). Chat messages are encrypted using AES-256 before being stored. We use HTTPS for all network communication. Only you can access your own private data — other users see only your public profile information.

🤝 Third-Party Services

  • Supabase — database, authentication, and file storage. Privacy Policy
  • Firebase / Google — push notifications (FCM) and Google Sign-In. Privacy Policy
  • Apple — Sign in with Apple authentication. Privacy Policy
  • RevenueCat — subscription and in-app purchase management. Privacy Policy

👶 Children's Privacy

Match&Play is intended for users aged 17 and older. We do not knowingly collect data from children under 17. If we become aware that a minor has created an account, we will delete it promptly.

🌍 Your Rights

  • Access: You can view all your profile data within the app.
  • Edit: You can update your profile at any time.
  • Delete: You can request full account deletion, which removes all your personal data, messages, and matches. Contact us at the email below.
  • Block/Report: You can block or report any user within the app at any time.
